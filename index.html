Our Brief History

The company was started in 2011 by Lucas Kgomo. who saw a business opportunity in the growing need of buiding double story houses. The trend came about in South African's realising that in order to extend their homes they needed to build up because there is little space within the yard. There was now a market for providing the most essential part of buiding up which is laying slabs and stairs.

Our Mission

To expand and spread our services accross the country to help home owners build their dream homes and help business and institues to reach greater heights
